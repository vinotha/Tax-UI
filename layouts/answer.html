Does rolling a 401(k) into a roth IRA disqualify an individual from the earned income credit if their actual earnings are within the income window?

Posted by consultant on 27th Nov 2013

No, earned income only includes all the taxable income and wages you get from working or from certain disability payments. Earned income does not include retirement, pay received for work while an inmate in a penal institution, interest and dividends, social security, unemployment benefits, alimony or child support as referenced in IRS Publication 596.
